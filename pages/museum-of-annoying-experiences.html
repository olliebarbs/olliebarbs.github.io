The Museum of Annoying Experiences

Zendesk

To highlight the strengths of Zendesk's products, we imagined a museum of the future dedicated to the customer service frustrations of of the past. The Web experience featured a museum space filled with gamified exhibits for users to explore and play with.

The experience featured a hybrid lighting system incorporating both PBR and pre-baked illumination maps, alloweing for different lighing states to be triggered during various points.
