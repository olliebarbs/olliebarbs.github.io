Louboutin AR Experience

Mytheresa / Louboutin

A web-based AR experience for Mytheresa in partnership with Louboutin.

The project showcased faithful reproductions of three different styles of Louboutin shoe and web-based AR experience triggered by the iconic Mytheresa packaging.

Given the high-end nature of the hero assets, this project required a great deal of attention to detail and accuracy. At the same time however, it needed to be lightweight in order to remain performant. To achieve this I used a combination of 3D photogrammetry, high-to-low poly modelling and texture baking.
