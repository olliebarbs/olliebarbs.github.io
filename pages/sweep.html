Carbon On Track

Sweep

Sweep’s carbon track tool gives their clients a fun and rewarding way to ensure their organisation is keeping up with their emission tracking commitments.

The web-based tool features a dynamic 3D island that evolves based upon user’s business type and their progress in tracking their organisation’s emissions.

As the lead 3D artist on the project I was responsible for crafting the overall illustrative style, conceptualizing, creating and animating assets for the tool and developing pre-rendered collateral to support the project.
