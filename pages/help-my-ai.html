Help My AI

Zendesk

Zendesk’s “Help My AI” is a cross platform campaign that imagines customer service AI as characters, emphasizing the challenges and difficulties they have compared to Zendesk's solutions.

The project involved creating four fully realised characters, of which I had the pleasure of developing, modelling and animating two; Derek and Serenit-E. I was responsible for contributing to the initial design of both characters, modelling, rigging and animating. Characters were modeled, and textured Blender, with animation in Blender and after effects.
